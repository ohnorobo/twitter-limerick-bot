
Word classes for our purposes:
STP - stopwords
DET - determiner
ADJ - adjective
VRB - verb
NOU - noun
ADV - adverb
PRO - pronoun
PRP - preposition
CNJ - conjunction
WHW - wh words
CTR - contraction


thoughts in POS and long lines
most of these start with an explicitly weak word, a/there/if/ etc
starting with a noun or a verb is probably no good


strong starts:
"There once"
stress on a verb with weak words before that
a multi-syllable word which controls the first stress
